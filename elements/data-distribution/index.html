Distribution

Recommendations to create effective visualizations within Ipreo products

Distribution Graph Types

Distribution analaysis visualizes data to see what data deviates from the norm and can also be used geographically to demonstrate data over a map. Charts used in this category include histograms, line and area charts, scatter plots, data tables, and map charts. Distribution graphs can answer:

  • What correlation between a cause and effect of a variable?
  • Does my user need to spot bugs or constraints in their system?
  • Does my consumer need a visual for a variable such as profits over different categories such as clients?
